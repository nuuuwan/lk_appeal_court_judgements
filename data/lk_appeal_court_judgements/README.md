A Court of Appeal judgment is a higher court ruling that reviews decisions of lower courts, shaping legal precedent and protecting citizens’ rights.

- [**10,495** documents](https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_appeal_court_judgements/tree/data/data/lk_appeal_court_judgements) (**10.6 GB**), from **2012-04-23** to **2026-02-27**, scraped from [https://courtofappeal.lk](https://courtofappeal.lk)

- In **JSON**, **PDF**, **TXT** & **🤗 Hugging Face**

- In **English**

- 🎓 Cite as **[arXiv:2510.04124](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.04124) [cs.CL]**
